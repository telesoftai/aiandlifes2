AI AND LIFE

This is an educational and informative event that aims at sharing knowledge about Artificial Intelligence and how it impacts our daily lives through timed training and workshops, currently events are oriented at The University Of Dodoma-(CIVE).

EVENT ORGANIZERS

The event is organized by Telesoft mainly Telesoftai in partinership with The University Of Dodoma,College Of Informatics and Virtual Education.Organizers are very interested in leveraging the potential of technology advancement for social development.

TELESOFT

Telesoft is the technology company currently running two products Telesoftai and Telesoftdev. These products are dependently running different activities such as Web,Desktop & Mobile development and AI Researches,training and Projects.

TELESOFTAI

Telesoftai is the product of Telesoft for activating Artificial Intelligence technology which provides integrity of AI driven to Telesoft, it mainly deals with Artificial Intelligence and its derivatives, visioned on making Telesoft a heart of AI technology in Africa.
